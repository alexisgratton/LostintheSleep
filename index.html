Tesseract Studios is a small game development studio comprised of game design students who attend Niagara College’s Game Development program. The third-year students are required to develop and design a video game of their choice for their senior year capstone project. This is the studio’s first time working together to develop and design a video game.

Lost in the Sleep is the first game by Tesseract Studios. Lost in the Sleep is a survival horror game with aspects of mystery and puzzles. The game takes place in the 1980s inside an old Georgian manor that was converted into an orphanage by a dark cult. They have been using the children housed inside for their rituals to appease their lord. In Lost in the Sleep, you play as an orphaned teen named Corina as she finds herself in a waking nightmare. With her brother missing, you must journey into nightmares of her fellow orphans to unlock the secrets of Hollow Orphanage’s dark past to save him. Be careful though, or you too may become lost in the sleep.
